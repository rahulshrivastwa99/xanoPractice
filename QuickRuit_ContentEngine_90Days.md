## PART 0: BRAND INTELLIGENCE REPORT

### What QuickRuit Is
**QuickRuit** (quickruit.in) is an AI hiring automation platform built for fast-growing Indian startups and SMEs. It automates the entire first round of hiring: bulk resume screening → AI-generated interview questions → async video interviews → candidate ranking. Free to start.

**Core Value Proposition:**  
*Screen, interview, and rank candidates — automatically, before you open a single inbox.*

**Brand Colors:** #2563EB (Electric Blue), White, Dark Neutral  
**Brand Voice:** Modern startup, intelligent, bold, human-centered, founder-led  
**Target Market:** Indian startups, founders, HR managers, recruiters, fast-growing SMEs  
**Positioning:** India's most accessible AI-first hiring automation tool — free to start, built for speed.

### Key Features (Inferred + Research-Backed)
1. Bulk resume screening (AI-powered, instant)
2. AI-generated interview questions (role-specific)
3. Async/one-way video interviews (no scheduling required)
4. AI candidate ranking + scoring
5. First-round hiring on autopilot
6. Free to start

### Pain Points QuickRuit Solves
- Founders spending 20+ hours/week reviewing resumes
- HR teams drowning in 200+ applications for one role
- Slow hiring causing talent loss to competitors
- Expensive recruiter fees for basic screening
- Inconsistent interview quality and bias
- No-show candidates wasting interview slots
- Lack of structured first-round evaluation

### Competitive Landscape (India Market)
- **Global competitors:** HireVue (enterprise, expensive), Spark Hire ($299/mo), Truffle ($149/mo)
- **India competitors:** Keka HR, Darwinbox, Freshteam (full ATS, complex)
- **QuickRuit's edge:** Free to start + AI-first + India-built + focused on first-round automation

### Market Data (2025-2026)
- 90% of Indian firms have piloted GenAI in HR
- 60% of recruiters use AI for resume screening
- 80% of Indian employers struggle to find right candidates
- AI can reduce hiring costs by 30% per hire
- Average Indian startup takes 45+ days to fill a role
- 93% of recruiters plan to increase AI usage in 2026
